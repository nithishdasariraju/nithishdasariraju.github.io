E-Health Record Management and AI-Powered Medical Prescription

Overview: The "E-Health Record Management and AI-Powered Medical Prescription" project revolutionizes healthcare workflows by integrating advanced Electronic Health Record (EHR) management with AI-driven personalized prescription recommendations. Built on Salesforce, this platform simplifies healthcare providers' access to secure patient data, ensures compliance with stringent privacy regulations, and enhances the accuracy of medical prescriptions through artificial intelligence.

This project not only modernizes traditional healthcare systems but also aligns with the increasing demands for personalized patient care, making a significant impact on both clinical operations and patient satisfaction.

Problem Statement: Healthcare systems often face challenges with managing large volumes of patient data securely and efficiently, leading to issues like data inaccessibility, prescription errors, and non-compliance with privacy regulations. The lack of streamlined workflows and personalized care solutions further hampers the ability of healthcare providers to deliver optimal patient outcomes. This project aims to address these challenges by integrating secure Electronic Health Record (EHR) management with AI-powered prescription recommendations, ensuring compliance, accuracy, and improved operational efficiency.

Key Features:

  • Streamlined Data Management: Leveraging Salesforce's capabilities, this project provides healthcare providers with a secure and user-friendly interface for storing, accessing, and updating patient records.
  • AI-Powered Prescriptions: Through OpenAIâ€™s API integration, it offers tailored medication suggestions by analyzing patient data, including medical history, allergies, and ongoing treatments, ensuring improved treatment outcomes.
  • Privacy and Compliance: Adheres to HIPAA standards for data security and ensures confidentiality with robust encryption protocols and role-based access controls.
  • Enhanced Efficiency: Agile development enabled continuous improvement, delivering a platform that minimizes prescription errors, enhances patient outcomes, and improves healthcare providers' operational efficiency.
